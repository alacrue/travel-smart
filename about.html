About Us

As it becomes easier and cheaper to travel, it becomes easier to pump carbon dioxide (CO2) into the atmosphere. Global CO2 emissions have increased 90% since 1970.

One of Earth's biggest offenders? The transportation industry.
Planes, trains, and automobiles all contribute greatly to our global greenhouse gas emissions.

  • Automobiles: Produce 19.6 pounds of CO2 per gallon of gasoline.
  • Planes: Produce 18.4-21.1 pounds of CO2 per gallon of fuel. Did You Know? A Boeing 747 can consume 1 gallon of fuel per second!
  • Trains: Produce 22.4 pounds of CO2 per gallon of diesel fuel.
